EchoVera Icon Generator

This tool generates all the icon sizes your PWA needs. Click any icon to download it, or use "Download All" to get them one by one.

💡 How to use: Click Generate All Icons below, then click each icon to download it. Save them all into a folder called icons inside your project folder alongside index.html.

📁 How to organize your files

Your project folder should look like this: echovera/
├── index.html    ← your app
├── manifest.json  ← PWA manifest
├── sw.js         ← service worker
└── icons/        ← create this folder
    ├── icon-72.png
    ├── icon-96.png
    ├── icon-120.png
    ├── icon-128.png
    ├── icon-144.png
    ├── icon-152.png
    ├── icon-180.png
    ├── icon-192.png
    ├── icon-256.png
    ├── icon-384.png
    └── icon-512.png
After saving all icons: 1. Upload everything to GitHub (index.html, manifest.json, sw.js, and the icons/ folder)
2. Vercel will automatically redeploy
3. Open your app on mobile → your browser will show an "Add to Home Screen" prompt!
On iPhone (iOS): Tap the Share button (box with arrow) → scroll down → tap "Add to Home Screen" → tap Add. EchoVera will appear on your home screen like a real app!
On Android (Chrome): A banner will automatically appear at the bottom of your screen saying "Add EchoVera to Home screen". Tap Install. Or tap the 3-dot menu → "Add to Home screen".
